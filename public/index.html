What is KovaLine?

KovaLine is a journaling application, connected to an Amazon Alexa for your convenience. At KovaLine, we directly designed our application to help you quit your addiction and track your progress. Quitting an addiction is one of the hardest things to do, but just know that when you do quit, you come out the other side stronger than ever. We're here to help you with your journey. This particular model was created for individuals with mental health issues that feed into their addictions.

How do I use Kovaline Alexa?

Simply say "Alexa, new journal entry". Alexa will then ask how you feel. If you have any other questions, you can ask "Alexa, how do I use this skill?" and she will tell you!
